# Product Overview

**Ademy** is an African-focused online learning platform mobile app built with React Native and Expo. The platform connects trainers with learners, enabling course creation, video-based learning, and progress tracking.
## Core Features
- **Dual User Roles**: Trainers create and manage courses; Learners browse, enroll, and consume content
- **Video-Based Learning**: Integrated with Bunny.net for video hosting and streaming
- **Progress Tracking**: Lesson completion tracking and course progress analytics
- **Multi-language Support**: French (default) and English with i18n-js
- **XAF Currency**: Central African CFA Franc for course pricing

## Target Market
African education market with focus on French-speaking regions, supporting both free and paid course models.

## Key Value Propositions
- Enables African trainers to monetize their expertise
- Provides accessible quality education content
- Mobile-first approach for widespread accessibility
- Localized currency and language support
